**Telegraf Samples**
These are sanitized examples for metrics and log shipping.

**Files**
- `github/telegraf/telegraf.conf.sample`
- `github/telegraf/telegraf.d/system-metrics.conf.sample`
- `github/telegraf/telegraf.d/logs.conf.sample`

**Usage**
- Copy the samples into `/etc/telegraf` on your server.
- Replace placeholders for VictoriaMetrics and Loki endpoints.
- Restart `telegraf`.

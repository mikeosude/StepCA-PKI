**Step CA Samples**
These are sanitized examples for Step CA configuration and service setup.

**Files**
- `github/stepca/ca.json.sample`
- `github/stepca/step-ca.service.sample`
- `github/stepca/step-ca.env.sample`

**Usage**
- Copy the samples into `/etc/step-ca` and `/etc/systemd/system` on your server.
- Replace placeholders in `ca.json.sample`.
- Reload systemd and start `step-ca`.
